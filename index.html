Division of Infectious Diseases, Emory University School of Medicine, Atlanta, Georgia


Population genomics of bacterial pathogens, focusing recently on Staphylococcus aureus and Chlamydia trachomatis. Roles of horizontal gene transfer and recombination in the evolution of virulence. Metagenomic analysis of clinical specimens and diagnostic design. Development of bioinformatic tools and databases for rapid genome analysis.
