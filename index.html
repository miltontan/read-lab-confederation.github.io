Tim Read's Lab

Professor, Division of Infectious Diseases, Emory University School of Medicine, Atlanta, Georgia

Population genomics of bacterial pathogens, focusing currently on *Staphylococcus aureus*, *Chlamydia trachomatis*. Roles of horizontal gene transfer and recombination in the evolution of virulence. Metagenomic analysis of clinical specimens. Functional genomic approaches to pathogen vaccine and diagnostic design. Development of bioinformatic tools and databases for rapid genome analysis. More coming soon!
